Advising Notes AI Assistant

This tools allows you to record a student meeting either by using the start button on the page for live transcription of your meeting or pasting meetings notes in the text box.

About the Advising Tool

Specifically, this can tool do the following:

  • Transcribe your meeting
  • Summarize a meeting
  • Pick up questions that were raised by the student
  • Answer these questions with updated information from the Lehigh Website
  • Follow up with the student with the meeting summary, list of question, answers, and potential action steps
